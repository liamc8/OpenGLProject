This is a guide for some of the controls for the executable file included in this zip file. 

W: Camera rotation in positive direction about Z-axis

S: Camera rotation in negative direction about Z-axis

Up Arrow: Camera rotation in positive direction about X-axis

Down Arrow: Camera rotation in negative direction about X-axis

Left Arrow: Rotation in negative direction about Y-axis

Right Arrow: Rotation in positive direction about Y-axis

Minus(-): Zooms camera out

Equals(=): Zooms camera in 

The functionality of the spotlight is fairly straightforward, it shines where the cursor is 
located on the screen and disappears when the cursor exits the window.

The executable is located in the folder titled "Final" in the root directory.
